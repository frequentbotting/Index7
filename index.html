Who We Are

Not a retreat.
A collection.

Solace Collection was built for people who have done the work — the therapy, the podcasts, the self-help books — and still feel like something is missing. That something is embodied experience. Real connection. Permission to feel it all.

Our Philosophy

Healing doesn't have to feel like hard work.

We believe the most profound transformations happen not in isolation, but in community. In rooms where everyone has chosen to show up fully. Around tables where the food is extraordinary and the conversation is real.

We blend the depth of intentional healing with the joy of being fully alive. Mornings of stillness, afternoons of adventure, evenings of laughter and connection. You leave not just lighter — but more yourself than you've been in years.
